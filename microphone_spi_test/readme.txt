Hardware Connections is as follows:
- Pin 10 (along PWM bar) is connceted to SS on the PModMic.
- On SPI Header, when using the "." as reference to bottom right of header looking down on it:
  - Bottom right is connected to MISO on the PModMic.
  - Center right is connected to SCK on the PModMic.
  - Top Left is connected to GND on the PModMic.
  - Bottom Left is connected to VCC on the PModMic
